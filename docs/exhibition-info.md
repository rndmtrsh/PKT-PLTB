# Exhibition Information
## Pameran PKT PLTB Archimedes Spiral 2026

## About the Exhibition

The **Pameran PKT PLTB Archimedes Spiral 2026** is a groundbreaking exhibition showcasing innovative wind power technology designed for Indonesia's renewable energy future. This interactive event demonstrates how ancient mathematical principles combined with modern engineering can create sustainable energy solutions for homes, businesses, and communities.

## Exhibition Theme

**"Harnessing Ancient Wisdom for Tomorrow's Energy"**

The exhibition explores how the 2,000-year-old Archimedes Spiral principle has been adapted into a revolutionary wind turbine design that addresses modern energy challenges while protecting the environment.

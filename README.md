# PKT-PLTB - Pameran Archimedes Spiral 2026

## Exhibition Overview
Welcome to the PKT Wind Power Plant (Pembangkit Listrik Tenaga Bayu) Exhibition showcasing innovative Archimedes Spiral technology for sustainable energy generation in 2026.

## About the Project
This exhibition demonstrates the application of Archimedes Spiral principles in modern wind power generation technology. The Archimedes Spiral blade design offers improved efficiency and reduced noise compared to traditional wind turbine designs.

## Key Features
- **Archimedes Spiral Blade Technology**: Revolutionary blade design based on ancient mathematical principles
- **Increased Efficiency**: Up to 30% more efficient than conventional wind turbines
- **Reduced Noise**: Quieter operation suitable for urban and residential areas
- **Sustainable Energy**: Clean, renewable power generation for the future

## Exhibition Details
- **Year**: 2026
- **Focus**: Archimedes Spiral Wind Power Technology
- **Theme**: Innovation in Renewable Energy

## Technology Highlights
The Archimedes Spiral design:
1. Utilizes a conical shape with spiral blades
2. Captures wind from multiple directions
3. Operates efficiently at lower wind speeds
4. Minimizes bird and bat mortality
5. Requires less maintenance than traditional turbines
